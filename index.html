Why do we care?
According to WHO (World Health Organization), there are 285 million people worldwide who, due to some disability, cannot read all content on a website.

Government regulations

  • Canada - Standard on Web Accessibility
  • Ireland – Disability Act 2005
  • Malta – The Foundation for Information Technology Accessibility
  • UK – Equality Act 2010
  • USA – Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA)
  • USA – Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act
  • Norway – Anti-Discrimination and Accessibility Act 2008

  • Organize in pairs

  • Switch positions with your pair
